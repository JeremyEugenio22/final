About US

What is JJAnime?

JJAnime is a legal streaming company that offers anime to viewers all over the world. Founded by students from the Polytechnic University of the Philippines, Sta. Maria Bulacan campus, JJAnime aims to bring high-quality anime to a global audience, legally streaming a wide range of genres including Action, Comedy, Romance, Historical, Shounen, Supernatural, and more. Whether you're new to anime or a lifelong fan, JJAnime provides a platform where you can immerse yourself in captivating stories, beautiful animation, and powerful characters.

Developers:

The company was founded by John Jeremy Eugenio, John Paul Balonzo, and Anthony Lumbao, who are second-year students pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines, Sta. Maria Bulacan campus. They are passionate about anime and committed to bringing a safe, legal streaming experience to fans worldwide. Their dedication to quality content and user experience shines through in the platform’s seamless streaming and up-to-date releases.

JJAnime is here to continue the growth of anime culture globally, providing a platform for fans to explore, enjoy, and share their love for anime.
